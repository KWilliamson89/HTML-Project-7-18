Who I am

My journey so far into software has been a very interesting one. It really began back in 2012 when I witnessed a really good friend teach himself how to code in one summer while I watched in the background while playing Halo Reach. I've flirted with the idea on and off for a few years now but never pulled the trigger.

After 2020 and how much of an effect that had on myself (and the world at large), I decided to take the time to finally learn. No longer could I deny the pull it had on me. Combine that with my voracious appitite to learn, I began to self-teach last year.

I have dreams of helping the unhoused in my community as well as making sure young aspiring artist (The kiddos!) have an outlet to express themselves safely. Learning software devlopement is a vehicle that I hope I can use to work towards that aspiration!

Skills I hope to learn
